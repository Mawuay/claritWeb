More on What We Do?

We Are In A Mission To Help The Helpless

Our Vision is to be an internationally recognised centre that promotes cutting edge research and training, empowers and safeguards the rights of the vulnerable by providing a platform for participation, discussion and influencing law and policy making.

Our objectives include; promoting research into relevant legal issues; promoting legal advocacy, raising awareness on the legal rights of the vulnerable, promoting knowledge development and exercise of legal rights; strengthening the capacity of vulnerable groups and; influencing the formulation of policies.
